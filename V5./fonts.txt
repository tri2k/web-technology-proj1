FONTS
place in head of html:

<link rel="preconnect" href="https://fonts.googleapis.com">
<link rel="preconnect" href="https://fonts.gstatic.com" crossorigin>
<link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Montserrat&family=Varela+Round&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">

place in css where needed:
font-family: 'Montserrat', sans-serif;     			(Main font)
font-family: 'Varela Round', sans-serif;				(title font)
